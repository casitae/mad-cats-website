A space for music, culture, conversation, and the people who bring them to life. To get involved or explore an idea, reach out to daniella@madcats.uk.

What We Host

An evening dedicated to attentive listening, where a record, artist or musical theme takes centre stage. Guided selections, shared discoveries and conversation come together in the spirit of the listening bar.

A live conversation with musicians, creatives and cultural figures — a closer look at the people, stories and ideas shaping music, hospitality and beyond.

Guest bartenders and hospitality teams bring their own perspective to Mad Cats — special menus, new flavours and a fresh interpretation of the space for one evening.

Community-led gatherings that bring people together around a shared cause — supporting meaningful initiatives through music, food and hospitality.

A showcase for independent designers, makers and brands whose work reflects the creativity and culture that inspires Mad Cats. Part retail experience, part community gathering.

A relaxed forum for readers to share ideas and conversation, each gathering centred around a selected title — over drinks and good company.

A celebration of London's thriving jazz community. As part of the festival programme, Mad Cats hosts performances and events that reflect the curiosity, diversity and spirit of contemporary jazz.
